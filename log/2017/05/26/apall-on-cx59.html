CX59

Information

Regarding CX59 (Hynes et al. 2012) says in section 6.14:

6.14. CX59 (CXOGBS J174500.5–261228) CX59 was matched with TYC 6832-663-1 with a quite large offset of 3.53 arcsec. This source was detected only 3 arcmin off-axis by Chandra with 27 photons, so there is no reason to expect such a large offset. This star has not been reported as a double, and the X-ray source is also the hardest of the sources considered here for which we can calculate a hardness ratio. We conclude that this is likely to be a chance alignment.
